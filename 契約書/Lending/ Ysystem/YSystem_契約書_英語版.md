# Software Development Agreement

This Agreement is made between **CURIOX SOLUTIONS LIMITED** (hereinafter referred to as "Party A") and **[Client Company]** (hereinafter referred to as "Party B"), concerning the outsourcing of software development services, as follows:

## 1. Purpose of Agreement

Party A shall outsource to Party B the development of software related to a banking (lending) system (hereinafter referred to as "the Services"), and Party B agrees to undertake such Services.

## 2. Definitions

The terms used in this Agreement shall have the following meanings:

1. **Software**: The program, content, database, and all related materials developed under this Agreement.
2. **Program**: The executable code and related modules of the Software.
3. **Deliverables**: All items developed by Party A and delivered to Party B under this Agreement.
4. **Original Materials**: Materials provided by Party B to Party A during the course of the Services.

## 3. Scope of Services

The Services shall include the design, development, testing, and delivery of the Software by Party A, based on specifications provided by Party B, until the Software operates in a functional state.

## 4. Delivery of Deliverables

Party A shall deliver the Deliverables to Party B within the agreed-upon deadline.

## 5. Fees and Payment

1. Party B shall pay Party A the outsourcing fee as separately agreed upon.
2. The payment method shall be determined through mutual consultation at the time of contract execution.

## 6. Change of Fees

If changes in specifications, delivery schedule, or errors in the materials provided by Party B cause Party A to incur additional costs, Party A may request a change in the outsourcing fee.

## 7. Provision of Materials

Party B shall provide Party A with, free of charge, the materials, information, and equipment necessary for the performance of the Services.

## 8. Management of Materials

Party A shall manage the materials provided by Party B with due care and shall not use them for purposes other than the Services. Party A shall retain backups of the development environment, technical documents, and source code for one year after termination of this Agreement.

## 9. Return of Materials

Party A shall return all materials provided by Party B within ten (10) days after the completion of the Services.

## 10. Development Location

Party A shall perform the Services at its business premises.

## 11. Contact Persons

Both parties shall designate contact persons for the Services and notify each other in writing, email, or other communication tools.

## 12. Confidentiality

Both parties shall strictly manage and not disclose or leak any confidential information obtained from the other party in connection with this Agreement, except as required by law. This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement.

## 13. Handling of Personal Information

Party A shall handle any personal information entrusted by Party B solely within the scope of this Agreement and shall not provide it to third parties without prior written consent from Party B.

## 14. Transfer of Ownership and Risk

Ownership and intellectual property rights of the Deliverables shall be transferred from Party A to Party B when Party B has fully paid the outsourcing fee.

## 15. Inspection

Party B shall inspect the Deliverables within twenty (20) business days of delivery and notify Party A of any defects. If no notice is given within such period, the Deliverables shall be deemed accepted.

## 16. Warranty and Liability

1. Party A warrants that the Deliverables conform to the agreed specifications and are free from defects.
2. The warranty period shall be one (1) year from the date of delivery.
3. If defects are found within the warranty period, Party A shall, at its own cost and responsibility, correct or repair the Deliverables.

## 17. Maintenance Services

After expiration of the warranty period, any maintenance or technical support shall be subject to a separate agreement.

## 18. Copyright

1. **Copyright of Newly Developed Features**
   All copyrights and ownership rights to newly developed features under this Agreement shall belong exclusively to Party B. Party B may freely use, modify, and license such Deliverables, including granting rights to third parties.

2. **Rights to Existing Technology**
   Party A shall have the right to use its existing technology, know-how, libraries, frameworks, and other intellectual property (hereinafter referred to as "Existing Technology") in performing the Services. Copyrights and ownership rights to Existing Technology shall remain with Party A and shall not be transferred to Party B.

3. **Exclusion of Generic Technical Elements**
   The following generic technical elements shall remain the property of Party A even if included in the Deliverables under this Agreement:
   - General algorithms and data structures
   - Standard programming techniques and design patterns
   - Open source libraries and frameworks
   - Reusable components developed by Party A for other projects
   - General user interface elements and layouts

## 19. Liability and Compensation

1. **Liability for Damages**
   Party A shall be liable for damages arising from infringement of third-party rights or defects in the Deliverables, provided that such liability shall be limited to 200% of the total amount of the outsourcing fee.

2. **Exclusion of Indirect Damages**
   Party A shall not be liable for lost profits, loss of opportunity, indirect damages, special damages, or punitive damages.

3. **Exemptions**
   Party A shall not be liable for damages in the following cases:
   - Damages caused by errors or deficiencies in materials provided by Party B
   - Damages arising from specification changes instructed by Party B
   - Damages caused by improper use or operation by Party B
   - Damages caused by unauthorized access or tampering by third parties

## 20. Term of Agreement

The term of this Agreement shall be from **[Date]** to **[Date]**.

## 21. Termination

1. **Termination for Material Breach**
   Either party may immediately terminate this Agreement without notice if the other party commits a material breach, suspends payment, enters into bankruptcy or similar proceedings, or otherwise becomes unable to perform this Agreement.

2. **Cure Period**
   In the event of a contract breach, the breaching party shall be given a 30-day opportunity to cure such breach after receiving written notice. If the breach is not cured within this period, the other party may terminate this Agreement.

3. **Settlement Procedures upon Termination**

   - Upon termination, Party A shall have the right to receive appropriate compensation for work already completed
   - Party B shall be obligated to pay for deliverables completed up to the termination date
   - Party A shall return all materials provided by Party B within ten (10) days after termination
   - Confidentiality obligations shall continue after termination

4. **Effect of Termination**
   Termination of this Agreement shall not relieve either party of obligations incurred prior to termination.

## 22. Non-Assignment

Neither party may assign its rights or obligations under this Agreement to a third party without the prior written consent of the other party.

## 23. Good Faith Consultation

Any matters not stipulated in this Agreement shall be resolved by mutual consultation in good faith between the parties.

## 24. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Japan, and the exclusive jurisdiction for any disputes arising hereunder shall be the courts of Tokyo, Japan.
