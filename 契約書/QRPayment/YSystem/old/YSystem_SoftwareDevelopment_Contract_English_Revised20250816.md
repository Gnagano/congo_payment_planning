# Software Development Agreement (Revised Version)

## JCFinances (QR Code Payment Feature)

---

This Software Development Agreement (the "Agreement" or "Software Development Agreement") sets forth the terms and conditions of the agreement between **CURIOX SOLUTIONS LIMITED**, with its principal place of business at Suite 2701-08 27/F., Shui On Centre, Nos. 6-8 Harbour Road, Wanchai, Hong Kong (the "Developer"), and **[Client Company Name]**, with its principal place of business at [Client Address] (the "Client").

**WHEREAS**, the Client has conceived of a QR Code Payment Feature (the "Software"), and the Developer is the contractor with whom the Client has agreed to develop the Software.

**THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants made by the Developer and the Client (individually, a "Party" and collectively, the "Parties") to this Software Development Agreement, the Developer and the Client agree as follows:

## 1. Developer's Obligations

- The Client hereby engages the Developer, and the Developer agrees to be engaged by the Client to develop the Software in accordance with the specifications.
- The Developer shall complete the development of the Software. The final product shall be delivered to the Client by **December 5, 2025**. The milestone dates listed in Exhibit B shall be binding. Failure by the Developer to meet a milestone by more than 10 business days shall entitle the Client to liquidated damages of 1% of the milestone fee per week of delay.
- For a period of **12 months** after delivery of the final product, the Developer shall provide assistance to the Client in answering questions or solving problems, strictly limited to the scope of the specifications set forth in Exhibit A. This includes training on the operation and integration of the QR Code Payment Feature, with unlimited remote bug fixes and training limited to a maximum of **40 hours** total. Additional support or training beyond this scope shall be provided under a separate service agreement at the Developer's standard rates.
- The Client may terminate this Software Development Agreement at any time upon material breach of the terms of this Agreement, provided such breach is not cured within **30 days** after notice.

## 2. Delivery

- The Software shall function according to the specifications on or before the delivery date.
- If the delivered Software does not conform to the specifications, the Client shall notify the Developer in writing within **60 days** of the delivery date, with a written description of the problems. The Developer agrees to make reasonable efforts to correct the non-conformance.
- The Client shall provide written notice of acceptance (or rejection) within **60 days** of the delivery date based on conformance to the specifications. Use in production shall not constitute acceptance unless and until the Client signs the Acceptance Certificate, which may be withheld until all critical defects are resolved.

## 3. Compensation

The Client shall pay the Developer for services according to the development phases set forth in Exhibit B. The payment amounts and timeline are detailed in that document. The dates for each development phase listed in Exhibit B shall be binding, and the Developer shall provide advance notice (approximately 2 weeks prior) and inform the Client of actual delivery dates based on development progress.

## 4. Intellectual Property Rights

Intellectual property rights related to the QR Code Payment Feature created specifically for this project (including but not limited to source code and documentation developed specifically for this project) shall be owned by the Client. However, the Developer retains ownership of existing intellectual property, general methodologies, know-how, and technologies that existed prior to this Agreement or are generally applicable to multiple projects. The Developer grants the Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use such existing technologies solely in connection with the delivered Software.

For existing technologies used in this project, including past project deliverables owned by the Client, mutual licensing for use in this project is granted.

## 5. Advance Notice of Delays and Agreement

1. The Developer shall notify the Client in writing at least 15 days before the expected delay date when milestone achievement is anticipated to be difficult.

2. The notification in the preceding paragraph shall include the following:

   - Cause and details of the delay
   - Expected delay period
   - Functions and scope affected
   - Proposed countermeasures and alternatives

3. The Client shall select one of the following within 10 business days of receiving the notification:

   - Acceptance of the delay (agreement on new schedule)
   - Adjustment of specifications and priorities
   - Phased delivery through partial delivery

4. When the Client accepts the delay, penalties for such delay shall not apply.

5. Delays due to force majeure, Client-initiated changes, or third-party system failures shall be exempt from penalties regardless of advance notice.

## 6. Specification Changes

The Client may make change requests through a formal change management process. Changes that result in substantial modifications to the project scope, timeline, or cost must be documented in writing and approved by both parties before implementation. The Developer shall provide a written estimate of additional time and cost within 5 business days of receiving a change request. Written approval from the Client is required before proceeding with changes that incur additional costs or extend the delivery timeline.

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business and technical information obtained from the other party during the course of this Agreement. This mutual obligation includes, but is not limited to, specifications, source code, business plans, methodologies, and know-how. Unauthorized use, disclosure, or reproduction of confidential information is not permitted. This confidentiality obligation shall remain in effect for 3 years after the termination or expiration of this Agreement.

## 8. Developer's Warranties

The Developer represents and warrants that:

- No existing agreements prohibit the Developer from entering into this Agreement.
- The Software will not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of third parties.
- The Software will operate according to the specifications for **one (1) year** from the acceptance date. Malfunctions during that period will be corrected at no additional cost, except for malfunctions caused by Client modifications, third-party integrations, or environmental factors beyond the Developer's control.

## 9. Indemnification

The Developer's total liability under this Agreement (including indemnification obligations) shall not exceed 200% of the total amount paid by the Client under this Agreement or USD 500,000, whichever is higher, except that this cap shall not apply in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct. The Developer shall fully indemnify and defend the Client against any third-party IP infringement claims arising from the Software, regardless of fault, except where the claim results from the Client's unauthorized modifications. The Developer's indemnification obligations shall not apply to Client modifications, integration with third-party systems not specified in the original requirements, or use of the Software outside its intended scope.

## 10. Modifications

Modifications to this Agreement shall be effective only if made in writing and signed by both parties.

## 11. Governing Law

This Software Development Agreement and the interpretation of its terms shall be governed by Japanese law, and the Tokyo District Court shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising under this Agreement.
