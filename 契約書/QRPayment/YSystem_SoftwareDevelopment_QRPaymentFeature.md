# Software Development Agreement
## JCFinances（QR Code Payment Feature）

---

This Software Development Agreement (the "Agreement" or "Software Development Agreement") states the terms and conditions that govern the contractual agreement between **CURIOX SOLUTIONS LIMITED**, having its principal place of business at Suite 2701-08 27/F., Shui On Centre, Nos. 6-8 Harbour Road, Wanchai, Hong Kong (the "Developer") and **[Client Company Name]**, having its principal place of business at [Client Address] (the "Client") who agrees to be bound by this Agreement.

**WHEREAS**, the Client has conceptualized a QR code payment feature (the "Software"), and the Developer is a contractor with whom the Client has come to an agreement to develop the Software.

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises made by the parties to this Software Development Agreement, the Developer and the Client (individually, each a "Party" and collectively, the "Parties") covenant and agree as follows:

## 1. Developer's Duties

- The Client hereby engages the Developer and the Developer hereby agrees to be engaged by the Client to develop the Software in accordance with the specifications.
- The Developer shall complete the development of the Software. The final product shall be delivered to the Client by **05 December 2025**.
- For a period **180 days** of development after delivery of the final product, the Developer shall provide the Client assistance in answering questions or resolving issues strictly within the scope of the specification described in Exhibit A.
- The Client may terminate this Software Development Agreement at any time upon material breach of the terms herein and failure to cure such breach within **180 days** of notification.
- The Developer shall provide the Client, if requested, a cumulative **180 days** of training regarding the operation and integration of the QR code payment feature after the Delivery Date.

## 2. Delivery

- The Software shall function in accordance with the Specifications on or before the Delivery Date.
- If the delivered Software does not conform to the Specifications, the Client shall notify the Developer within **180 days** of the Delivery Date with a written description of the issues. The Developer agrees to make reasonable efforts to correct any non-conformity.
- The Client shall provide a written notice of acceptance (or rejection) based on conformity to the Specifications within **180 days** of the Delivery Date.

## 3. Compensation

The Client shall pay the Developer for the services as per the development phases described in Exhibit B. Payment amounts and timelines are detailed therein. The dates listed in Exhibit B for each development phase are for reference only. The Developer will provide advance notice (approximately two weeks prior) and inform the Client of the actual delivery dates based on the progress of development.

## 4. Intellectual Property Rights

All intellectual property rights related to the QR code payment feature, including but not limited to source code and documentation, shall be solely owned by the Client. The Developer waives any rights to claim ownership.

## 5. Change in Specifications

Reasonable change requests may be made by the Client. If such changes require additional effort or may cause delay, the Developer shall inform the Client and await approval to proceed with the changes under adjusted timelines and/or additional compensation. The Developer's judgment regarding delay or cost implications shall be respected.

## 6. Confidentiality

The Developer agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business and technical information (including specifications, source code, and business plans) obtained from the Client. No unauthorized use, disclosure, or duplication of confidential information is permitted.

## 7. Developer Warranties

The Developer represents and warrants that:
- No existing contract prohibits the Developer from entering into this agreement.
- The Software will not infringe on any third-party intellectual property rights.
- The Software will operate according to the Specifications for **180 days** after delivery. Any malfunction during that time will be remedied at no extra cost.

## 8. Indemnification

The Developer agrees to indemnify and defend the Client against any claims, including legal fees, arising from intellectual property infringement or breach of warranty.

## 9. Modification

No modification of this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 10. Governing Law

This Software Development Agreement and the interpretation of its terms shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **Japan (Tokyo)** and subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the federal and state courts located in **Japan, Tokyo**.
